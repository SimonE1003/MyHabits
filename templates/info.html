Procedure

  1. Think about 6 habits you want to form, in which you can do consistently
  2. Bracket those into 3 time - phases (morning, afternoon and evening)
  3. Go to the “Set Habit” page to start your 21 - day challenge
  4. Aim to complete 4 - 5 habits every day (it's great if you can do all six, but it's not required)
  5. If you missed some habits, don't compensate the next day! Just focus on completing the 4 - 5 habits next day!
  6. The result will appear in the “Today” page after 21 days!

Sample Habits

Note:

  • Below are some habits that I think everybody could benefit from, they are all cost-free and rooted in science and biology.
  • The time phases are only for reference, you can still benefit from performing certain habits in different time phases.

Morning

(Alert and focused state: Action and Focus oriented)

  1. Sunlight viewing for 5-20 min
  2. Hard Work for 1.5h
  3. Visualize the day (to reduce limbic friction)
  4. Cold Exposure (Cold shower)
  5. Physical Exercise

Afternoon

(Highest Serotonin, Relaxed state of being)

  1. Meditation
  2. Brainstorming
  3. Talking to loved ones

Evening

(Relax yourself and prepare yourself for sleep)

  1. Reading
  2. Journaling
  3. NSDR (non sleep deep rest)
  4. Dim the lights
